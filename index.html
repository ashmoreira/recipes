chicken and broccoli alfredo

ingredients

  1. 1 lb boneless chicken breast
  2. 2 heads of broccoli
  3. 300 g pasta of choice
  4. 1 jar of alfredo sauce
  5. 5 cloves of garlic
  6. 1/2 onion
  7. 2 tbsp olive oil
  8. 1 teaspoon of butter
  9. salt and pepper to taste
  10. 1 teaspoon of garlic powder, paprika, and onion powder
  11. 2 teaspoons of red pepper flakes
  12. 1 teaspoons of tapitio

directions

  1. cut broccoli into small florets and fill a pot with water. start to boil broccoli with salt while prepping other vegetables and chicken
  2. mince garlic and onion and set aside to cube chicken into bite-size pieces. grab a pan and heat up the olive oil
  3. add chicken and all seasonings to the pan and cook until golden brown, then add garlic and onion
  4. once broccoli is fork tender, drain and set aside while filling that same pot with water and salt for pasta
  5. add broccoli and butter to cooked chicken and stir on low heat
  6. after a minute or two, add alfredo sauce to the pan with chicken and garlic mixture, add salt, pepper, red pepper flakes to taste. let simmer on low heat
  7. once water is boiling, add pasta and cook for 10 minutes or until al dente
  8. drain pasta, and add to the sauce pan and stir.
  9. serve on a plate and enjoy!

chicken japanese curry

  • 1 lb boneless chicken breast
  • two large carrots
  • 1 pack of white mushrooms
  • 1 pack of golden curry (i use extra hot)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • two cups of sushi rice

    • directions

    1. wash rice well, and add four cups of water to rice cooker and start cooking it
    2. dice carrots, mushrooms, and onions, mince garlic and set aside
    3. cube chicken into bite size pieces. grab a pot and heat up the olive oil
    4. add chicken and all seasonings to the pan and cook until golden brown, then add garlic, onion, carrots, and mushrooms.
    5. once onion and carrot soften, add enough water to pot to cover everything in it and let cook
    6. once every ingredient is cooked, add curry paste to the pan i use about 3 pieces and a small mesh strainer to easily dissolve it.
    7. let simmer on low heat until sauce thickens
    8. once rice is cooked, fluff with a rice paddle.
    9. serve curry on top of rice and enjoy!

    picadinho

    ingredients

    1. 1 pound of ground beef
    2. three medium potatoes (i use yellow)
    3. 1 onion
    4. 5 cloves of garlic
    5. 1 & 1/2 cups parboiled rice
    6. 1 can of sliced beets
    7. 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
    8. optionally: 1/2 cup of olives
    9. salt and pepper to taste
    10. 2 tbsp olive oil
    11. a pinch of fresh parsley
    12. 1 teaspoon of garlic powder, paprika, and onion powder
    13. 2 teaspoons of red pepper flakes
    14. 1 teaspoons of tapitio

    directions

    1. dice potatoes, onions, and mince garlic and parsley
    2. prep rice by adding about 1/5 of garlic and a handful of diced onion with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, olive oil, and parsley to a small pot
    3. cook down ground beef with all powder seasonings and tapitio in a pan until 80% cooked then add potatoes, onions, and garlic
    4. add enough water to pot to cover everything and then add tomato sauce and let simmer at medium heat
    5. start cooking rice on medium heat for about 10 minutes or until rice is mostly dry then remove from heat and let sit for at least 5 minutes
    6. once potatoes are fully cooked remove the sauce pot from heat and let sit for about 5 minutes as well.
    7. serve picadinho on top of rice and enjoy!
